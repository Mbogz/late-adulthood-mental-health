Late Adulthood

Learning Objectives

After review of this website, the learner will be able to:

  • Describe key lifestyle characteristics of late adulthood after one review session.
  • Explain major mental health concerns affecting older adults.
  • Analyze why these mental health issues emerge in late adulthood.
  • Match mental health concerns with appropriate adaptation mechanisms.
  • Apply age-appropriate support strategies when interacting with older adults.
